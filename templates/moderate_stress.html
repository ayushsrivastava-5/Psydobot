Helpful Suggestions for Reducing your Stress Level

( Moderate Stress Level )



ASMR Video

ASMR, or autonomous sensory meridian response, produces a tingling feeling throughout your body. Different audio and visual triggers, such as whispering, fingernail tapping, or watching a brush stroke a surface, can provoke this tingling sensation. Aside from just making you feel good, ASMR also has the potential to help with feelings of anxiety. According to a research conducted by the Ohio State Univeristy, ASMR videos can slow heart rate, leading to a state of relaxation and calm.




Padangusthasana ( Big Toe Pose )

This yoga pose helps in calming the brain and relieve stress and anxiety ,stimulates the liver and kidneys, stretches the hamstrings and calves, strengthens the thighs, improves digestion, relieve the symptoms of menopause and relieve headache and insomnia





Simple Breathing Techniques


Breathing exercises can be a great way to help relieve stress and anxiety.The breathing techniques mainly include Square breathing, which is so good at helping to relieve stress and anxiety that it is sometimes used in the military to help calm nerves before stressful situations, and Pursed breathing, which is another breathing technique that can help relieve stress and anxiety, and it can also help make our lungs stronger.
